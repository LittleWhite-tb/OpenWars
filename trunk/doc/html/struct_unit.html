Detailed Description

Unit struct.

Keeps the following:

  • The enum to characterise the unit (id)
  • The category of the unit
  • The category of the attackable unit
  • The movement possibility
  • The fuel
  • The fuel consumption per day
  • The ammo
  • The life of the unit
  • The price
